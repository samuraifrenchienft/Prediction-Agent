# Kalshi — User Guide for EDGE Agent Reference

## What is Kalshi?
Kalshi is a US-regulated prediction market exchange (CFTC-regulated).
Unlike Polymarket (crypto/decentralized), Kalshi is a traditional financial exchange
that accepts USD directly. Users trade event contracts — YES/NO on real-world outcomes.

Kalshi is legal for US users (unlike some offshore platforms).

---

## How to Sign Up

1. Go to kalshi.com
2. Click "Sign Up"
3. Enter email, create password
4. **Identity verification required** (KYC — Kalshi is regulated):
   - Submit government-issued ID (passport or driver's license)
   - May take a few minutes to a few hours
5. Must be 18+ and a US resident (or eligible international user)

**No crypto wallet needed** — Kalshi is fully USD-based.

---

## How to Deposit

Kalshi accepts USD only (not USDC or crypto).

### Option A — ACH Bank Transfer (free, 3-5 business days)
1. Go to Account → Deposit
2. Link your US bank account (checking/savings)
3. Enter amount → confirm
4. Funds available in 3-5 business days
5. **Free — no deposit fee**

### Option B — Wire Transfer (faster, small bank fee)
1. Go to Account → Deposit → Wire
2. Kalshi provides wire instructions
3. Send from your bank
4. Arrives in 1-2 business days
5. Your bank may charge a wire fee ($15-30)

### Option C — Debit Card (instant, fee applies)
1. Go to Account → Deposit → Card
2. Enter debit card details
3. Instant deposit
4. Fee: typically 1.5-2.5%

**Minimum deposit**: $1

---

## Fees

- **Trading fee**: Kalshi charges a small fee on winning trades (~7% of profit on most markets)
- **Deposit fee**: Free via ACH, small fee for card
- **Withdrawal fee**: Free
- **No spread markup** — Kalshi makes money from the trading fee, not the bid-ask spread

---

## How Kalshi Contracts Work

Same mechanics as Polymarket but USD-denominated:
- Each contract trades between $0.01 and $0.99
- Pays $1.00 if correct, $0 if wrong
- Price = implied probability (e.g., $0.55 = 55% chance)

Example: "Will inflation be above 3% in December?" at $0.42 YES
- Buy YES at $0.42: profit $0.58 if inflation >3%, lose $0.42 if not

---

## How to Trade

1. Browse markets at kalshi.com/markets
2. Click a market → choose YES or NO
3. Enter number of contracts (each contract = $1 max payout)
4. Review total cost → click "Buy"
5. Order executes immediately if market price matches

**Categories on Kalshi**: Politics, Economics, Finance, Weather, Sports, Tech, Culture

---

## How to Withdraw

1. Account → Withdraw
2. Withdrawals go to your linked bank account (ACH)
3. Takes 2-3 business days
4. **No withdrawal fee**

## Kalshi vs Polymarket — Quick Comparison

| Feature | Kalshi | Polymarket |
|---|---|---|
| Regulated | Yes (CFTC) | No (decentralized) |
| Currency | USD | USDC (crypto) |
| US Users | Fully legal | Legal but complex |
| KYC | Required | Not required |
| Deposit method | Bank/card | Crypto/card |
| Fees | ~7% of winnings | 0% |
| Speed | Slower deposits | Instant (crypto) |
| Anonymity | No | Partial (wallet) |

## Tips for Kalshi Users

- ACH deposits are free but slow — fund in advance of anticipated trades
- The 7% fee on winnings means you need >53% accuracy to break even at 50/50 odds
- Kalshi markets tend to be more liquid on US political and economic events
- Use /scan to see Kalshi markets where EDGE has detected edge opportunities
- Tax reporting: Kalshi sends 1099 forms — keep records of all trades
